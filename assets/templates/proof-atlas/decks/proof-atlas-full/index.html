Limitations CANDOR REGISTER

What these findings cannot claim

Four limitations are recorded openly. None invalidate the headline result; each bounds it.

LimitationL-01
Sample skews to early-adopter regions.
Results may overstate effect for slower-onboarding regions; weight before extrapolating nationally.
LimitationL-02
Observation window is two quarters.
Durability of gains beyond six months is projected, not yet measured.
RiskL-03
3.1% of exceptions remain unresolved.
Held out of headline figures; could shift verified-outcome rate by up to 1.4 points.
CaveatL-04
Audit panel and program share one data feed.
Independence is procedural, not architectural; a third feed is recommended for the next review.
Source · limitations register PA-M-04 · reviewed by audit panel SRC-3310
