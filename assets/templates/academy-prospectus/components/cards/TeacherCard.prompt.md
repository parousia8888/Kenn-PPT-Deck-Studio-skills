An editorial faculty profile card — documentary portrait over a serif name, mono discipline, and a short bio. Avoids the generic centred-avatar look.

```jsx
<TeacherCard
  name="Dr. Elena Voss"
  discipline="Director · Linguistics"
  bio="PhD Cambridge. Twenty years shaping how language is taught."
  image="/faculty/voss.jpg"
/>
```

Use in a 3–4 column roster. Omit `image` to show a documentary placeholder.
