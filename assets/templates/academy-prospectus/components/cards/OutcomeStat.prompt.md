A credible outcome statistic — large serif figure, plain caption, and a sourcing line that keeps it honest.

```jsx
<OutcomeStat value="94" unit="%" caption="reached their target exam grade within two terms" source="Cohort 2024–25 · n=312" />
```

Inherits the section `--accent` for the number colour. Always include a `source` — credibility over hype is a core rule of the system.
