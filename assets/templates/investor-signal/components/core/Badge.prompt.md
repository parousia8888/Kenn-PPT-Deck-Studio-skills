Small mono status / stage chip for labels like "Series A", "Live", "Pilot".

```jsx
<Badge>Seed</Badge>
<Badge variant="accent" dot>Live revenue</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid">Q3 2025</Badge>
```

- `variant`: `outline` (default) · `accent` (filled, marks signal) · `solid`.
- `dot` adds a status indicator. Reserve `accent` for genuine signal.
