One-line mono uppercase label for eyebrows above claims and source/footnote lines on data pages.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Why now</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="muted">Source: company data, audited · FY24</Eyebrow>
```

- `tone`: `accent` (signal, default) · `muted` (sources/footnotes) · `secondary`.
- Use sparingly — one eyebrow per claim. Keeps the deck disciplined.
