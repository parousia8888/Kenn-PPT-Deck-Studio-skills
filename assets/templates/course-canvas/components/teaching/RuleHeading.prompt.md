Section heading built from a mono eyebrow, a short oxblood mark, and a serif title — the standard way to open a teaching section.

```jsx
<RuleHeading eyebrow="Key concept" title="Normal distribution" />
<RuleHeading eyebrow="Case breakdown" title="Reading one result" variant="sage" as="h1" />
```

`variant`: `ox` (default) or `sage`. `as` sets the heading level.
