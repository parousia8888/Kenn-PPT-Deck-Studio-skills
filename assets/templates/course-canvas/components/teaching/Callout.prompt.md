Bracketed aside for teaching notes — a left accent rule plus a small mono label.

```jsx
<Callout variant="sage" label="In plain words">
  Most results land near average; the further out, the rarer.
</Callout>
```

Variants: `ox` (oxblood, default — emphasis), `sage` (correct / example), `note` (neutral ink), `straw` (highlight / interpretation).
