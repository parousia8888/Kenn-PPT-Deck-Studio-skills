What to take away

HANDOUT · PRINTABLE
1
The normal curve is symmetric and set by two numbers — the mean μ and spread σ.
2
The 68–95–99.7 rule tells you how much data sits within 1, 2 and 3 σ.
3
A z-score rewrites any value as “how many σ from the mean”.
KEY TERMS · GLOSSARY
mean μ
the balancing center of the data
std. dev σ
the typical distance from the mean
z-score
(x − μ) ÷ σ — distance in σ units
empirical rule
68 / 95 / 99.7 within 1 / 2 / 3 σ
