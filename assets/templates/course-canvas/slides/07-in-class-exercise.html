Q1
A bag of flour is labelled 1000 g. The filling machine has μ = 1000 g and σ = 12 g. A bag weighing 976 g is 2 standard deviations below the mean.
Q2
Using the 68–95–99.7 rule, about  % of bags weigh between 988 g and 1012 g.
Q1: z = (976 − 1000) / 12 = −2. Q2: about 68%.
