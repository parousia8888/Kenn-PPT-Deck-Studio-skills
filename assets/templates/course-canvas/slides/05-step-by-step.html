STEP-BY-STEP

Turning a value into a z-score

  1. Find the centerWrite down the mean μ of the dataset.
  2. Measure the gapSubtract the mean from your value: x − μ.
  3. Scale by spreadDivide by the standard deviation σ.
  4. Read the resultThe z-score is how many σ from the mean you are.
Worked once
z = x − μ σ
x = 86   μ = 74   σ = 8
z = (86 − 74) / 8 = 1.5
Teaching cue Pause after step 3 — have students predict the sign of z before you reveal the number.
