# Style Archetypes

Use this reference when selecting or deriving a style. It prevents style names
from collapsing into vague labels such as "premium", "tech", or "minimal".

Each archetype must lock palette, type, density, graphics, motion, components,
and forbidden habits before a Gate 1B sample is created.

## Investor And Memo

### Founder Memo Minimal

- Palette: white or warm-white paper, black ink, one blue accent.
- Type: Windows-safe sans, direct title weight, mono metadata.
- Density: medium; one argument per slide, evidence rows not dashboard soup.
- Graphics: decision table, wedge map, KPI row, risk/action ledger.
- Motion: row-by-row reveal, no theatrical camera movement.
- Avoid: gradient orbs, playful badges, busy SaaS dashboards.

### Terminal Finance

- Palette: dark or paper terminal, cyan/amber deltas, restrained red/green.
- Type: mono metadata plus clear sans body.
- Density: high; every panel must be inspectable.
- Graphics: ticker row, table, heat map, compact line/bar chart.
- Motion: scan/reveal by row; B mode must freeze all data.
- Avoid: fake hacker green, ticker overload, unreadable microtext.

## Data Media And Editorial

### Financial Times Data Paper

- Palette: warm data paper, dark teal/ink, burgundy or orange emphasis.
- Type: editorial title, readable body, mono only for source metadata.
- Density: medium-high; every claim has source/annotation.
- Graphics: ruled chart, source note, editor annotation, data table.
- Motion: soft paper motion, chart draw, no UI-panel theatrics.
- Avoid: beige low contrast, decorative magazine clutter, generic cards.

### Economist / Policy Brief

- Palette: crisp white/paper, red or cobalt accent, neutral rule system.
- Type: authoritative serif or restrained sans, compact captions.
- Density: high but ordered; chart + annotation must dominate.
- Graphics: small multiples, slope chart, issue map, policy matrix.
- Motion: minimal, almost static.
- Avoid: glossy brand color, emotional hero treatment.

## Swiss And Information Design

### Swiss Signal Grid

- Palette: white/near-white, black, one hard signal color.
- Type: sans, strong hierarchy, no ornamental display.
- Density: medium-high; grid owns alignment.
- Graphics: matrix, band, axis, square dot, hard rule.
- Motion: grid lock-in, axis reveal.
- Avoid: shadows, rounded cards, pills, decorative gradients.

### Basel Research Grid

- Palette: cool white, graphite, technical blue/green accents.
- Type: research sans with mono labels.
- Density: high; evidence is modular and aligned.
- Graphics: coordinate, crosshair, table, citation rail.
- Motion: subtle layer reveal, no glow.
- Avoid: friendly SaaS surfaces and floaty objects.

## Education And Prospectus

### Course Canvas

- Palette: warm paper, editorial accents, high readability.
- Type: serif for teaching title only when style allows; sans for body.
- Density: medium; concept, example, exercise, recap each get distinct roles.
- Graphics: path, checklist, margin note, exercise box.
- Motion: paper-light, step reveal, B mode.
- Avoid: childish stickers, random black panels, over-round cards.

### Academy Prospectus

- Palette: institutional paper, navy/sage/maroon/gold by chapter.
- Type: calm serif/sans pairing with Windows fallback.
- Density: medium; trust proof needs room.
- Graphics: curriculum ladder, outcomes evidence, teacher proof, checklist.
- Motion: brochure page light, image reveal, no flashy AI HUD.
- Avoid: fake heraldry, low-contrast parchment, stock-card brochure sameness.

## Launch And Product

### Cinematic Keynote

- Palette: black/graphite or clean light stage, one luminous accent.
- Type: large light-to-semibold sans.
- Density: low-medium; one object or claim owns the slide.
- Graphics: product frame, capability strip, availability close.
- Motion: stage reveal, optical light, slow semantic entrance.
- Avoid: dense classroom text, random black panels, neon HUD.

### Hardware Blueprint

- Palette: technical blue/white or graphite/cyan, precise contrast.
- Type: sans + mono spec labels.
- Density: medium-high; modules and specifications are inspectable.
- Graphics: traces, pins, module boxes, benchmark table.
- Motion: trace draw, module lock-in.
- Avoid: fake circuit wallpaper, RGB gaming visuals.

## Evidence And Legal

### Proof Dossier

- Palette: paper/ink with coordinate blue or oxidized orange.
- Type: dossier serif/sans/mono roles; source notes visible.
- Density: high but governed by evidence cells.
- Graphics: ledger, risk marker, evidence grid, timeline.
- Motion: stamp, reveal, source attach.
- Avoid: friendly SaaS cards, decorative blobs, anonymous panels.

### Legal Brief

- Palette: off-white, black, restrained red/blue.
- Type: brief-like serif/sans, clear clause labels.
- Density: high; argument map and risk table.
- Graphics: exhibit box, clause row, timeline.
- Motion: near-static, page-turn or rule reveal only.
- Avoid: playful cards, neon, gradient.
